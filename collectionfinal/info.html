about the collection

i made a blog on tumblr once and gave it a theme i liked, then messed with that theme a bit to suit how i wanted it to look. the end result worked... up to a certain point. scrolling too far down created a glitch that combined posts at random intervals. that's how i found the pieces you see on this site: these 100 images are screenshots of the glitch as it created new art out of the raw content it was fed.
